About Me

I'm Elena Vision, a professional photographer with over 10 years of experience capturing moments that matter. My approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to create images that tell compelling stories.

My work has been featured in numerous publications and exhibitions worldwide. I specialize in portrait, wedding, and commercial photography, with a passion for capturing the essence of every subject.

Based in New York, I'm available for assignments across the globe. Let's collaborate to create imagery that resonates with your audience and stands the test of time.
